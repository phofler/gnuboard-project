Modern Minimalist Villa in Seoul

By Admin • 2024.01.15 • Views: 1,234

Nestled in the heart of Seoul, this minimalist villa represents the perfect harmony between modern brutalism and natural tranquility. The design philosophy centers around the concept of "Void," creating spaces that allow light and shadow to dance freely throughout the day.

The Concept of Void

Unlike traditional architecture that focuses on filling space, this project prioritizes emptiness. The large concrete walls act as canvases for the changing sunlight, while the strategically placed windows frame the surrounding nature like living paintings.

The materials used are raw and honest. Exposed concrete, natural oak wood, and glass create a palette that is both sophisticated and grounded. Every detail has been carefully considered to ensure a seamless flow between the interior and exterior spaces.
